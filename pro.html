-An Efficient Method to Discover Rare Patterns Using Hyper-Linked Data Structure

-Data Mining project

A new technique is discussed to extract rare patterns from data set which is more time efficient compared to existing approach and the effectiveness of the approach is measured. ...
Rare pattern mining has emerged as a compelling field of research over the years.Hyper linked data structure proposed in the paper was inefficient in terms of time and memory. In my study, an efficient rare pattern mining technique was proposed that employs a hyper-linked data structure to overcome the shortcomings of hyper linked data structure proposed in the paper. The efficient hyper-linked data structure technique enables dynamic adjustment of links during the mining process that reduces the space overhead, reduces the number of scans and performs better with sparse datasets.
